Orthodontic Dentistry

Dr. Xu had extensive training in orthodontics through Progressive Orthodontics, so we offer comprehensive orthodontic treatment, which includes interceptive ortho treatment, invisalign, metal braces, six month braces.

Call us at (217) 356-3335 for a free consultation!

Ortho treatment flow chart:

  1. Free consultation
  2. Record appointment, we will take x-rays and pictures and or impressions for the record
  3. Braces placement
  4. Monthly visit (6-8 weeks)
  5. Braces off, retainer made
